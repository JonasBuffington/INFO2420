Website Critique

Utah National Parks

www.utah.com/destinations/national-parks

Q/A:

  • Q: What was your first impression of this site?
    A: It seems organized and professional.
  • Q: How does this site organize the flow of information?
    A: All of the links are on the left side which helps for organization and easy navigability of the site.
    in the center is larger buttons that take you to the same place as the links, they have nice images of the parks.
  • Q: Does the site structure make sense or does it confuse you? Why?
    A: It makes sense. Every element seems well placed and thought out, everything has a reason to be where it is.
  • Q: Does the site graphics/layout/multimedia enhance the site or detract from it? Why?
    A: I think it definitely enhances the site. It adds good visual flair which would not be present if the site did not have graphics.
  • Q: How long did it take for the home page to load? Was the load time reasonable?
    A: The load time of most of the elements was good, but the images loaded quite slowly, which I'm sure could be improved.
  • Q: Was the text color and size readable? If a background color or image was used, did it enhance or detract from the site?
    A: The elements are very readable and normal. No visual elements on the site strike me as ugly or detract from the site.
  • Q: Did the site have a navigation bar or other navigation tool? Was the navigation well done?
    A: The site has a good navigation bar, it seems to contain links to necessary information.
  • Q: Did the site have a color scheme or look and feel that was repeated on all the pages?
    A: Yes, the color scheme and style are consistent across all the pages, and it feels appropriate and well put together.
  • Q: How does the site respond to smaller window sizes?
    A: The site responds well to smaller window sizes, it keeps every element visible and readable.
  • Q: What does this site offer that was unusual or very well done?
    A: I think something this site did well was making all of the information that people are most likely to be looking for very accesible.
    The main page immediately presents information that I believe most people who are accessing the site would want to see first.
